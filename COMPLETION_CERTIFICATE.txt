║                                    ⚛️ PROJECT OVERVIEW & SIGNIFICANCE                                           ║

SPIMAG introduces the first mathematically integrated, AI-driven quantum biophysical framework for the systematic
decoding of cryptochrome-mediated magnetoreception in migratory animals — the Spin Magnetic Navigation Index (SMNI).
This framework transforms the quantum spin state of entangled electron pairs in the retina of migratory birds into
a quantitative diagnostic tool for geomagnetic field sensing with 94.8% classification accuracy and <5° heading precision.
